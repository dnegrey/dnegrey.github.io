About

My name is Dan Negrey and I am a data scientist based in Cleveland, OH. My career has centered around marketing analytics but I am genuinely interested in all things data, especially when it involves the use of open-source technology. In particular, I love using R with Shiny and R Markdown to build powerful data tools, dashboards and reports. I studied mathematics at Ohio University where I earned a Master of Science degree in 2007.


My approach

Over the years I have developed a consistent approach to tackling data science problems, which centers around four key components and emphasizes reproducibility:

  1. Getting data (querying an API or database, scraping a website, importing flat files, etc.)
  2. Cleaning data (scrubbing, keying, joining, filtering, etc.)
  3. Analyzing data (exploring, aggregating, modeling, visualizing, etc.)
  4. Publishing results (knitting a report, updating a dashboard, emailing a summary, etc.)


A few of my favorite things

When not conversing about data science, you may find me opining about sports, video games, science fiction and popular culture. I am also a proud husband, father, animal lover and vegetarian.
